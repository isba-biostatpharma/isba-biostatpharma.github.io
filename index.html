Our Mission

The aim of this specialized section of ISBA is to help network and federate under a common well-known “brand” all the initiatives to spread Bayesian methods and ideas, to solve problems in biosatistis and pharmacuetical statistics.

  • workshops
  • short courses
  • continuing education
  • sessions within larger conferences, etc.

We also want to help bridge academic and industry in this regard: there are plenty of people on both sides with excellent ideas and stimulating problems, and this section can help connecting them, bringing visibility from each to the others, and overall to all.
